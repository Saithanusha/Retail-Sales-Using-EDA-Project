#  Retail Sales Performance & Product Category Analysis

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to identify **high-performing product categories** and understand **customer purchasing behavior** for better business decision-making.

##  Business Objective
- Identify product categories contributing the most to total revenue  
- Analyze sales patterns and high-value transactions  
- Support inventory planning and promotion strategies

## Dataset Description
- Retail transactional dataset (1000 rows, 9 columns)
- Includes customer details, product categories, quantity, pricing, and total sales amount

##  Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  

##  Analysis Steps
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Feature understanding and filtering  
- GroupBy and pivot table analysis  
- Descriptive statistics  
- Data visualization (bar charts, histograms)

## Key Insights
- Electronics and similar categories are major revenue drivers  
- A few high-value transactions significantly impact total sales  
- Revenue concentration highlights potential business risks

##  Business Impact
- Helps businesses focus on top-performing categories  
- Supports data-driven inventory and promotion decisions  
- Encourages revenue stabilization strategies

##  Conclusion
This project demonstrates practical **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** skills and the ability to convert raw data into meaningful business insights.
